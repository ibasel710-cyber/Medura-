FAQ

Questions?
We've Heard Them All.

Q: Is my patient data safe?

A: Yes. We use Supabase PostgreSQL with row-level security — each clinic's data is isolated at the database level. Files are stored on Cloudflare R2 with access-controlled URLs.

Q: What if I already have patient records somewhere else?

A: We'll help you migrate. Most clinics have Excel files or old system exports — we handle the import as part of onboarding.

Q: What happens if the internet goes down?

A: Medura Hub is web-based. Internet is required for real-time features. However, the platform is designed for minimal latency, and a mobile hotspot is more than enough.

Q: Can I add more doctors later?

A: Yes. Add doctors at any time. Billing adjusts based on your plan.

Q: I'm not tech-savvy. Is this hard to learn?

A: The most common feedback from receptionists: "It's easier than I expected." We'll train you and your team before you go live.

Q: What's the minimum commitment?

A: Annual plans only. But with a 30-day full refund, you have a risk-free first month to evaluate.
